Leading the Way

We all contribute to the success of PK’s safety program: from executives fostering our safety culture, to superintendents leading the way, project coordinators who help trade partners understand our safety practices, and tradespeople guiding young apprentices.

Safety Stats

We maintain best-in-class industry safety with over 50 million hours worked annually and an overall total recordable incident rate and lost-time frequency rate among the industry’s lowest.

Continuous Improvement

Our people are our most valuable asset. We are dedicated to a safe and healthy work environment for all. We constantly look for innovative ways to improve practices and work with partners to embrace a mindset of uncompromising safety.

Safety Innovation

We evolve our safety practices by embracing technology in both the office and on job sites. Our Health, Safety and Environment group partners with the business technology group to introduce new safety applications.

Hazard Inspection

The Hazard Inspection app blends machine learning with safety standards to conduct inspections. By using photos of hazards and descriptions, it reduces manual entry and helps us identify and mitigate safety incidents.

Pre-job Safety Instruction

The Pre-job Safety Instruction (PSI) and PSI Audit apps let users complete and audit safety instructions with ease. By giving access to documentation and easy data capture, PSI reduces manual entry and saves valuable time.
